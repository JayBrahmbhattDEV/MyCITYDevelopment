About Us

The application is designed to improve the citizen engagement in India eventually serving as a bridge between the government and its citizens. The citizens will be able to register their issues such as litter, broken footpaths, potholes etc. all of which could be defined under public works undertaken by the municipal corporation. The application also has the potential to create employment opportunities and at the same time cater to its citizens.

The application on opening will ask new users for basic information like name, email and phone number after which the app will lead the user to its homepage. Once on the homepage the user can easily add reports and view reports.
